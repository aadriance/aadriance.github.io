Description

MonsterBuild is a passion project for a friend and I. We started programming in the third grade using a simple drag and drop programming tool called GameMaker 6. Over the years GameMaker has moved away from being a simple drag and drop learning tool and into a more professional software package. However, we wanted to create a project that kept the spirit of a simple game making drag and drop tool alive. Our goal isn't to give kids fundamanetal knowledge of for loops or if statments. Our goal is to show kids what it can be like to create, and have the power to bring the things in their head to life on screen. That is what got my friend and I into programming, and we hope to give that to a new generation.

Highlights

  • Developed for iOS using Swift
  • Interpreted programming language backend
  • Uses XCTests
  • Developed and maintained for over two years
